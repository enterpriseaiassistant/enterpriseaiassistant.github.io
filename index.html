Enterprise AI

AI prototypes designed and built by Srinivasa Karri (LinkedIn), focused on AI-driven enterprise systems and intelligent automation.

These three solutions form a connected AI-powered support ecosystem: Onboarding Assistant prepares users to get started correctly, Enterprise Assistant provides real-time guided support, and Ticket Intake Automation improves backend efficiency by automating ticket intake and routing.
