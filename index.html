Featured Project

Enterprise Assistant

An AI-powered prototype that transforms incoming support requests into structured tickets and actionable workflows.

  • Problem: Support requests often arrive as unstructured emails, making triage slow and inconsistent.
  • Solution: Use AI to extract the issue, identify the application, classify the request type, and prepare ticket content automatically.
  • Systems involved: Outlook, AI processing layer, Google Sheets logging, ServiceNow/Jira routing.
  • Key capabilities: issue summarization, confidence scoring, routing logic, structured output generation, audit logging.
  • Goal: reduce manual intake effort and speed up support operations.
