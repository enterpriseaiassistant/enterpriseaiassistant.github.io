Enterprise Assistant

AI support prototype for enterprise workflows

Overview

Enterprise Assistant is an AI prototype focused on improving how enterprise support requests are handled. It helps structure requests, guide workflows, and reduce manual effort in support operations.

Current Problem

  • Support processes can be inconsistent and depend heavily on manual interpretation.
  • Requests may arrive in unstructured formats, making triage slower.
  • Teams spend time gathering information before action can begin.

How It Helps

  • Introduces structure to incoming support requests.
  • Improves consistency in how issues are understood and processed.
  • Creates a clearer path from request intake to action.
