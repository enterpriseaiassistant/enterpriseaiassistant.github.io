Ticket Intake Automation

AI-powered email-to-ticket automation prototype

Overview

Ticket Intake Automation is an AI-powered prototype that processes incoming support emails from start to finish with minimal manual intervention. It reads the email, extracts issue details, classifies the request type, determines whether Jira or ServiceNow should be used, creates the ticket, notifies the requester, assigns the work, and records the full audit trail.

Key Challenges

  • Unstructured support requests: Incoming emails require manual review to understand the issue and extract the details needed for ticket creation.
  • Inconsistent routing decisions: Teams must decide whether each issue belongs in Jira or ServiceNow, which can vary by person and slow intake.
  • Repetitive manual follow-up work: Ticket creation, requester notification, work assignment, and audit logging are repetitive tasks that consume support time.

How It Helps

  • Automatically reads incoming support emails and extracts key issue details.
  • Classifies the request and routes it to Jira or ServiceNow based on the issue type.
  • Creates the ticket and sends a confirmation email with the ticket number.
  • Assigns the work and records actions in an audit log for visibility and traceability.
