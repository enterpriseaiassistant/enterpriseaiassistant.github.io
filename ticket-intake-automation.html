1

Overview

  • End-to-end automation: Automates support intake from incoming emails through issue extraction, ticket creation, and assignment.
  • AI-driven triage: Uses AI to identify issue details and determine the appropriate handling path.
  • Operational efficiency: Improves consistency and efficiency across the intake process.
2

Key Challenges

  • Unstructured requests: Support emails require manual review to identify the issue and extract the right details.
  • Duplicate ticket creation: Users submit ServiceNow tickets, and the technical team then creates an associated Jira ticket.
  • Missing ticket trail: Some issues are handled directly through email without a logged ticket, reducing traceability and making later follow-up harder.
  • Delayed acknowledgment: Users do not receive acknowledgment until a technical resource begins working on them.
  • Manual intake effort: Ticket creation and assignment rely on repetitive manual work that slows intake processing.

Business Value

  • Eliminates duplication: Removes manual duplicate ticket creation between ServiceNow and Jira.
  • Improves traceability: Ensures issues are logged, tracked, and auditable.
  • Accelerates response: Speeds acknowledgment and intake handling through automated processing.
  • Reduces manual effort: Lowers repetitive work for support and technical teams.
5

Technology Used

  • OpenAI API: Issue understanding and structured classification
  • Make: Workflow orchestration and automation
  • Outlook: Intake mailbox and notification emails
  • Jira Cloud: Application-related ticket creation
  • ServiceNow: Support ticket creation
  • Google Sheets: Assignment mapping and audit logging
