Overview

Ticket Intake Automation is an AI-powered intake interface that processes support emails end-to-end by extracting issue details, classifying requests, routing them to Jira or ServiceNow, creating tickets, assigning work, notifying users, and maintaining a complete audit trail. When requests lack sufficient detail, it proactively collects the required information.

Key Challenges

  • Unstructured support requests: Incoming emails must be reviewed to understand the issue and extract the information needed for ticket creation.
  • Inconsistent routing decisions across systems: Determining whether a request belongs in Jira or ServiceNow varies by individual and slows down intake.
  • High volume of repetitive, low-value tasks: Ticket creation, requester communication, assignment, and audit logging require repeated manual effort.

How It Helps

  • Uses AI to extract structured issue details from unstructured email requests.
  • Classifies and routes requests to Jira or ServiceNow based on issue type.
  • Automates ticket creation, user notification, and assignment without manual intervention.
  • Maintains audit logs to support visibility, traceability, and operational monitoring.
