Overview

Onboarding Assistant is an AI-powered interface that guides users through onboarding in a structured and consistent manner, provides step-by-step assistance, and helps ensure onboarding is completed efficiently.

Technology Stack Used

OpenAI GPT
RAG
ServiceNow

Key Challenges

  • High volume of repetitive onboarding tasks: Onboarding requires repeated execution of the same steps, making the process time-consuming and inefficient.
  • Delays for users during onboarding: When onboarding is not streamlined, users experience confusion and slower completion of required steps.

How It Helps

  • Guides users through structured onboarding steps to ensure consistency.
  • Reduces repetitive manual effort for common onboarding activities.
  • Improves clarity for users throughout the onboarding process.
  • Enforces a structured flow to ensure onboarding is completed correctly.
