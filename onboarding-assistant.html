Onboarding Assistant

Guided onboarding support prototype

Overview

Onboarding Assistant is a prototype designed to help users move through onboarding steps more easily by providing guided assistance, reducing confusion, and making the process more consistent.

Current Problem

  • Users may not know the right sequence of onboarding steps.
  • Important setup tasks can be missed or delayed.
  • Onboarding experiences may vary depending on who provides support.

How It Helps

  • Guides users step by step through onboarding tasks.
  • Reduces dependency on manual back-and-forth communication.
  • Improves consistency and clarity across the onboarding journey.
